I'm a second year B.S. Social Psychology major, Literary Arts minor at UC San Diego with an extensive background in the arts. I graduated from the San Diego School of Creative and Performing Arts in 2024 with a major in creative writing after specializing in poetry, creative non-fiction, and serving as editor-in-chief of the school magazine for 3 years.

My achievements include 2 Gold Key certificates and 2 Silver Key certificates from The Alliance for Young Artists & Writers in March 2024, awarded in recognition of my aptitude in poetic and short narrative styles, and a published anthology titled Love Triumphs.

My Mission

As the daughter to two Filipino immigrants and an immigrant myself, I believe everyone deserves to feel a sense of belonging no matter where they've chosen to call home.

This June, I was blessed with the opportunity to work with incoming UCSD first years from low-income, underrepresented, and underserved backgrounds, helping them transition to the fast-paced quarter system and navigate their new journey with healthy mindsets. In doing so, I've grown exceptional conflict mediation and peer educator skills.

I carry that restorative spirit everywhere with me, whether that's UCSD's Kaibigang Pilipino club or in my writing. I write to foster healing within immigrant communities and help readers better connect to the people and land around them.
